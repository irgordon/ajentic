Glossary

Adapter
The local connector shape AJENTIC expects before a provider path can be used.
Provider
The local source that returns output for review.
Invocation
A bounded local request to the allowed provider path.
Validation
A check that data has the expected shape, linkage, and status.
Review
The operator-facing view of checks, findings, and boundaries.
Staged proposal
Possible candidate material prepared for validation before a local decision.
Operator decision
A local record of operator intent. It does not authorize actions.
Local candidate output
Candidate output created locally after required checks. It is not production approval.
Trial package
A local package of runbook, workflow, evidence, and boundary summaries.
Evidence
Local observations and summaries for review. Evidence is not authority.
Restore
A local preview of session information read back from package and evidence summaries.
Replay/status
A local summary of the recorded decision timeline and integrity state.
Stop condition
A local marker that tells the operator to stop and review the situation.
Escalation guidance
Descriptive guidance for who or what to review next. It does not activate authority.
Boundary
A limit that says what AJENTIC does not do in the local beta.

This glossary is explanatory, local-only, and non-production.
