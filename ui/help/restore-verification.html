Restore and verification

Read-back validation checks that local package and evidence data can be read back in the expected shape.

Restore preview shows what a restore view would display from local session information.

Replay/status shows the local decision timeline and integrity summary.

Replay/restore verification compares local workflow, package, evidence, and restore summaries so mismatches are visible.

verification does not repair replay. Verification does not promote recovery.

Restore and verification are local-only and non-production.
