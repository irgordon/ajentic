Validation, review, and candidates

Validation checks whether local data has the expected shape and linkage.

Review shows the operator what was checked, what is blocked, and what needs attention.

A staged proposal is possible candidate material that still needs staged validation and review.

An operator decision records local intent about a validated staged proposal. It does not authorize actions.

Local candidate materialization creates local candidate output only after the required local checks and decision path.

Local candidate output is not production approval. It is local-only and non-production.

Provider output remains untrusted throughout validation and review.
