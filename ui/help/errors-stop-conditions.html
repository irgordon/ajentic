Errors, stop conditions, and escalation guidance

Blockers show why the local workflow cannot continue.

Errors describe failed validation, rejected input, read-back failures, missing evidence, or mismatch drilldowns.

Mismatch drilldowns show where local package, evidence, replay, restore, or workflow summaries do not line up.

Stop condition observations tell the operator that a local stop marker was seen.

Stop conditions are observed and described, not automatically enforced.

Escalation guidance tells the operator who or what to review next. It is descriptive and does not activate authority.

These error and blocker views are local-only and non-production.
